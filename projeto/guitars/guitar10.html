Gibson

Les Paul Classic 2019 GT

  • Body: Mahogany
  • Top: Maple
  • Neck: Mahogany
  • Fretboard: Rosewood (Dalbergia latifolia)
  • Trapeze fretboard inlays
  • Cream-coloured binding
  • Neck profile: Slim taper
  • Scale 628 mm
  • Nut width: 43 mm
  • 22 medium frets
  • Pickup: Burstbucker 61T and 61R humbuckers
  • 2 Volume controls with push/pull function for coil tap
  • 2 Tone controls push/pull function for Pure Bypass/Phase
  • ABR-1 bridge
  • Aluminium stop bar
  • Grover machine heads
  • Colour: Gold Top
  • Made in USA

The new 2019 Gibson Les Paul Classic boasts a traditional tone wood construction of a mahogany back and maple top coupled with a mahogany neck and bound rosewood fingerboard. A slim taper neck profile and 61 R and 61 T zebra pickups provide the playability and classic Gibson tones from the era, 4 push-pull control assembly give a sonic variety to this complete guitar package.
