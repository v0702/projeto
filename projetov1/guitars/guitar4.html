Fender

American Special Strat HSS OW

  • Alder body
  • Maple neck
  • Modern C-neck profile
  • Rosewood fretboard (Dalbergia latifolia)
  • 22 Jumbo frets
  • Scale: 648 mm
  • Nut width: 42.8 mm
  • Pickups: 2x Texas Special single coils and 1x Atomic humbucker
  • Greasebucket tone circuit
  • Synchronized tremolo
  • 70s Headstock
  • Original strings: Fender NPS 250R .010 - .046 (Article Nr 104112)
  • Colour: Olympic White
  • Incl. gig bag

The Fender American Special for a long time has been the go to for those that want the best Fender USA has to offer but don't need all of the bells and whistles. This version of the American Special comes loaded with a set of HSS pickups which only makes it better for harder rock styles.
